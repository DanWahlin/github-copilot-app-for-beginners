# Code of Conduct

This course community should be welcoming, practical, and respectful.

## Expected behavior

- Be kind and constructive.
- Assume good intent when reviewing beginner questions.
- Explain technical feedback clearly.
- Respect different experience levels, tools, operating systems, and accessibility needs.
- Keep shared examples privacy-safe.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, insults, threats, or personal attacks.
- Sharing private information without permission.
- Publishing tokens, credentials, customer data, or private repository details.
- Dismissing beginner questions or shaming mistakes.
- Repeated off-topic or disruptive comments.

## Reporting

Report concerns through the repository maintainers or the contact path listed by the project owner. Do not include private information beyond what is needed to understand the concern.

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or restrict participation when needed to keep the project safe and useful.
